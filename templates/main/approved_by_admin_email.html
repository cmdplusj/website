To learn about how to use the platform, check out our getting started guide and some best practices. But if you're in a hurry, here's a quick reminder on how Command+J works, as well as some brief do's and don'ts on the platform:

How it works 🤖

We'll send an Intro

When a mentee reaches out to you, we send you a mail with the mentee's intro. You can then schedule some time to have the call with the mentee.

Call Scheduled 

Once It's scheduled you'll both be CC'ed in an email thread, where you can communicate before the call. Talk about the medium of the call, go over what topics are to be discussed, and so on.

Have the Call

Connect over whichever medium works for both you and your mentee. Do make sure you show up on time and don't ghost them - our community is all about mutual respect and reliability.

Tell us how it went

We’ll collect feedback after your connection. This is crucial as it helps us provide you more relevant matches in the future.

Dos and Don'ts 👀

The best rule of thumb is to treat Command+J like a professional environment. Be courteous and respectful to anyone you meet and interact with.

 

✅ Do try to respond as quickly as you can, so your mentee hears from you.

✅ Do join the meeting promptly on time. It's perfectly fine to wrap up a bit early, if you have a busy day ahead.

❌ Don't reschedule unless absolutely necessary.

❌ Don't ghost. Even if you have a last-minute engagement, let your mentee know. Never leave them hanging!

We hope you have many enriching and exciting interactions with mentees through Command+J. Oh, and don't forget to follow @cmdplusj on Instagram and Twitter to keep up with the latest developments.
