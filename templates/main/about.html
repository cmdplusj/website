About this Initiative!


Command+J is an attempt to create the open, inclusive and approachable forums; that we know the Indian Design Community deserves!

Our Vision


Our aim is to open-source information from the creatives, for the creatives! Knowledge should not be stuck in inadvertant confines which arise due to location, design education or social pull among other things.

We want to make meeting new people from your field of design as easy as walking into a cafe and talking to someone, and through that, cultivate a community of amazing indian designers.
