FAQs

Have a question and don’t see it here? Reach out to us

Who can be a mentor?
Regardless of if you’re a student, professional or an AI which has passed the turing-test, if you’ve walked a certain path… there’s a good chance that someone else out there is walking the same (approximate) path, and they could sure use some help!
Which is why being a mentor is open to everyone.
What kind of commitment does this require?
We all have busy lives, that’s why we want the mentoring process to be as flexible as possible. Whether it’s a quick 15-min call, an in-depth portfolio review or a catch-up email with a mentee after a long time, it’s what works best for you.
You can read more about the time commitment this would require here.
How do I stay in touch with my mentee?
Let's consider 2 scenarios -
1. You could have your first call with your mentor and decide on a path forward together through regularly scheduled calls.
2. You could reach out to each other as and when you want to - to catch up or otherwise.
Of course, not all mentor/mentee relationships need to last long. Sometimes it’s okay to just talk once if there’s no need for continuity.
What if I don’t want to match with a mentee?
Even though our vision is to make each connection work out… Due to the nature of human relationships, that’s a hard thing to get right. If for some reason you don’t want to get matched with a certain mentee again you can let us know at cmdplusj@gmail.com or through the feedback email that we’ll send you after every call.
